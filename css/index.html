Biography of Virat Kohli

Early Life

Virat Kohli was born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi into a Punjabi Hindu family. His father, Prem Kohli, worked as a criminal lawyer and his mother, Saroj Kohli, is a housewife.

Virat Kohli's style and consistent performance have made him one of the most influential cricketers of his generation. He continues to inspire many young athletes worldwide.
